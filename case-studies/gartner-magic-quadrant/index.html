Overview

Led the design effort for demo vignettes that became a critical artifact in Workday's sales cycles, contributing to Workday Financials achieving the #1 position in Ability to Execute in Gartner's 2025 Magic Quadrant for Cloud ERP for Service-Centric Enterprises.

"The demo vignettes transformed how our sales teams showcase Workday Financials capabilities, providing compelling, scenario-based demonstrations that resonate with enterprise buyers."

The Challenge

Enterprise sales cycles for Cloud ERP solutions are complex and highly competitive. Sales teams needed compelling demonstration materials that could:

  • Showcase Workday Financials' unique capabilities in realistic business scenarios
  • Differentiate from competitors like Oracle, SAP, and Microsoft in head-to-head evaluations
  • Enable consistent, high-quality demos across global sales teams
  • Address specific pain points that resonate with CFOs and finance leaders

My Role

Design Leadership

  • Led end-to-end design of demo vignettes for Workday Financials
  • Collaborated with Product Marketing, Sales Enablement, and Product teams
  • Created scenario-based demonstrations highlighting key differentiators
  • Ensured visual consistency and compelling storytelling across all vignettes
  • Iterated based on feedback from field sales teams and customer reactions

Approach

Understanding Sales Needs

Worked closely with sales enablement to understand the most critical moments in the sales cycle where demos make or break deals. Identified key scenarios that demonstrate Workday's strengths in financial management, planning, and analytics.

Scenario-Based Design

Developed vignettes around realistic business scenarios that CFOs and finance teams encounter daily, making the demonstrations immediately relatable and compelling for enterprise buyers evaluating Cloud ERP solutions.

Visual Excellence

Ensured every demo vignette showcased Workday's modern, intuitive interface while highlighting the depth of functionality that sets it apart from legacy ERP systems and competing cloud solutions.

Outcome

#1 in Ability to Execute

Workday was positioned highest in Ability to Execute in the 2025 Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for Cloud ERP for Service-Centric Enterprises. The demo vignettes became a top-performing artifact in sales cycles, helping sales teams effectively communicate Workday's value proposition to enterprise buyers.
